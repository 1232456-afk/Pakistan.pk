Overview

About Pakistan

Officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan — a sovereign nation in South Asia with rich history, diverse culture, and strategic importance.

Pakistan is the fifth-most populous country with 241 million people and world's second-largest Muslim population. Capital: Islamabad. Largest city: Karachi.

Total area 881,913 km² — 33rd largest. Bounded by Arabian Sea (south), India (east), Afghanistan & Iran (west), China (northeast).

Pakistan means "Land of the Pure" — from Pak (pure) + Stan (land). Name coined by Choudhry Rahmat Ali in 1933.
